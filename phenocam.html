PHENOCAM

A PHENOCAM (a specialized camera for monitoring phenology) has been deployed on the ULM Carbon Tower in the Russell Sage Wildlife Management Area. It captures seasonal changes in the bottomland hardwood forest canopy from 34m (~110 feet) high, surrounding the tower.
